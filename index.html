Why Vet Novel?

Organic

Vet Novel is created out of organic material such as coconut oils and natural components. Vet Novel is researched by Associated Professor of Veterinary Medicine Faculty Dr. Amnart Poapolathep and a team of researchers from the Department of Pharmacology of the Kasetsart University.

Hypoallergenic

Vet Novel is an innovative shampoo for dogs prone to or suffering from skin allergies. Vet Novel is a hypoallergenic formula that can be used on dogs of all breeds and all ages.

Clean & Moisturizing

This gentle solution cleans effectively, washes off easily and is delicate enough to keep your hands soft. Our special formula was developed because of our love and care for improving the lives of our canine friends. It moisturizes your dog's skin making the coat softer and shinier.
